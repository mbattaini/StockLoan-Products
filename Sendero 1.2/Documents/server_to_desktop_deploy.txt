Sendero Deployment 
-------------------------

To Start:

1)       You need to create a sendero folder on your c:\, C:\Sendero.

2)       Also you need to create a C:\Sendero\Logs directory.

3)       Next open an internet explorer window and type in the following address: \\SERVER_NAME\Bin\

4)       Copy the file Sendero.cmd from SERVER_NAME to C:\Sendero\.

5)       Next right click on C:\Sendero\Sendero.Cmd and click Create Shortcut.

6)       Double click on C:\Sendero\Sendero.Cmd, this will run the script and downlaod the executable.

7)       Now right-click on the shortcut just created. This will open the properties screen.

8)       Click Change Icon…

9)       Click Browse and open C:\Sendero\Anetics.MedalistClient.exe.

10)       Select the medalist icon and click ok.

11)       Now right click on the shortcut and select cut.

12)     Paste the icon on your desktop.

13)     Now double-click on the sendero shortcut.

14)    This will download all the executable files to your computer and start Sendero.
